EP Application Help Guide

About

Welcome to the engineering for professionals application help guide.

This is a brief and interactive session to demonstrate the application process for our part time online graduate program at the Whiting school of engineering. Be sure to hover your mouse over the drop mark image to reveal the application checklist. And yes, we really do only require transcripts.

Some programs may require a bit more. So please use the website to reference your specific program major next click on the menu and let's get started.

Application Instructions

  1. Read all application instructions carefully.
  2. Select Engineering for Professionals (Whiting Part-Time).
  3. Your application reference number is listed at the top left corner of each application page. Please include this number in any correspondence with EP admissions staff.
  4. This message is sent after you accept your offer and submit all official transcripts.
  5. Transcripts
    1. Your official transcript(s) must be received to enroll
    2. A final confirmation email will be sent
    3. Visit our webpage for instructions to submit transcripts

Status Update

The student's status page can be used for the following application steps:

  1. To upload additional transcripts
  2. View your decision letter
  3. Hopefully accept your offer

If you have a provisional offer, you have the option to take courses at JHU for a significantly reduced tuition cost

Provisional Status

We evaluate your prerequisites

  • We assign prerequisites in the application evaluation.
  • B- or better must be earned.
  • Complete the prerequisite application page.
  • Prerequisites must be completed prior to program courses

More about provisional status

  • Accept offer within one (1) year of applying
  • 5 year time requirement starts once you begin your first course as a fully accepted student
  • On average, students take up to one (1) year to complete their prerequisites
